How to Play

  1. Start the Quiz:

    Click the "Start Quiz" button to begin.

  2. Read the Question:

    The quiz will display a question on the screen.

  3. Select an Answer:

    Click on one of the answer options provided below the question.

  4. Check the Result:

    The screen will display whether your answer was correct or incorrect.

  5. Proceed to the Next Question:

    Click the "Next Question" button to move on to the next question.

  6. Complete the Quiz:

    Continue answering questions until you reach the end of the quiz.

  7. View Your Score:

    Once you've answered all the questions, your final score will be displayed on the screen.
